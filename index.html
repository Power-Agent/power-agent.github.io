Introduction

Power Agent is an open-source community dedicated to accelerating the development of LLM-powered tools and agentic workflows in the power systems domain. We aim to bridge the gap between cutting-edge AI and the real-world needs of energy professionals, researchers, and operatorsâ€”by creating a shared ecosystem of protocols, models, and workflows tailored for power applications.

PowerMCP

A protocol enabling LLMs to control and interact with power domain tools

PowerFM

Foundation models fine-tuned or trained from scratch for power system tasks

PowerWF

Modular agent workflows for orchestrating models, tools, and decisions

Motivation

The rapid rise of LLMs and agentic frameworks has opened new frontiers in intelligent software. Inspired by the success of open-source initiatives in LLMs (such as DeepSeek, LLaMA) and multi-agent control protocols (like Anthropic's Claude and Smithery), we believe it's time to bring the same spirit of innovation and openness to the power systems domain.

Community Curated

Like the best open-source software, Power Agent is built by and for the community. GitHub was chosen as the home of this initiative to enable transparent, global collaboration.

Curated by the Power and AI Initiative (PAI) at Harvard University, all contributions are reviewed to ensure they meet the highest standards of relevance, utility, and integrity. We invite researchers, engineers, and developers across disciplines to take part in shaping this ecosystem.
